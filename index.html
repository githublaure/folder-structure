About this website

Why is there so few girls Grandmaster playing chess? However, due to a lack of role models and few opportunities to compete on the same level as boys, there is a disproportionately low number of female Grandmasters in the world of chess. This is not a reflection of intelligence, but a consequence of societal norms that have discouraged many girls from pursuing the game.
